## Architecture Decisions
- CQRS: Neo4j (write) + TimescaleDB (read) — enforced, never mix
- Append-only ledger: JournalEntry/LedgerLine never updated/deleted
- Bi-temporal on every JournalEntry: valid_time + transaction_time
- Configuration over code: all policy via ConfigurationService
- Ontology boundary: hard learning wall between FINANCIAL and MISSION

## Phase 4 — AI Engine Services (IN PROGRESS)
- [x] P4-WEIGHT-LEARNER: AI weight learner with ontology-aware back-propagation
  - recordRealization: records realized_delta on Outcome, triggers back-propagation
  - Back-propagation: gradient descent on CONTRIBUTES_TO edges up to 6 hops
    - w_new = w_old + lr × hop_decay × (accuracy - 1)
    - Hop decay: learning rate attenuated by 1/hops for distant edges
  - Ontology boundary enforcement: edges crossing FINANCIAL/MISSION boundary skipped
  - Calibration updates: exponential smoothing (α=0.3) on accuracy per (entity, outcome_type)
  - Effective stake: ci_point_estimate × state_discount × calibration × ci_width_penalty
  - Effective contribution: weight × temporal_value_pct × control_score
  - Value state transitions: FORECASTED→ESTIMATED→VALIDATED→REALIZED with gates
    - ESTIMATED→VALIDATED requires epistemic_activity_id
  - Calibration history query and current factor retrieval
  - Kafka events: REALIZATION_RECORDED, EDGE_WEIGHT_UPDATED, VALUE_STATE_TRANSITION
